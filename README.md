## 📌 Project Overview

The Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard helps analyze retail sales performance by presenting key business metrics, customer insights, regional analysis, and sales trends in an easy-to-understand format.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to enable business users to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track revenue, cost, and profit
- Compare sales across product categories
- Analyze regional performance
- Identify valuable customers
- Observe sales trends over time

---

## 📂 Dataset

The project uses a retail sales dataset containing:

- Order Details
- Customer Information
- Product Categories
- Sales
- Cost
- Profit
- Quantity
- Region
- Country
- Ship Mode
- Order Date

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Sales
- 💵 Total Cost
- 📊 Total Profit
- 👥 Customer-wise Sales
- 🌍 Sales by Country
- 📦 Quantity by Region
- 🛍️ Sales & Profit by Sub-Category
- 📅 Year-wise Order Trend

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Interactive Ship Mode Filter
- Customer Performance Table
- Country-wise Sales Map
- Regional Quantity Distribution
- Sales & Profit Analysis by Sub-Category
- Year-wise Sales Trend

## 💡 Business Insights

- Identifies high-performing product sub-categories.
- Compares sales performance across different countries.
- Highlights customer purchasing patterns.
- Tracks sales trends over multiple years.
- Supports business decision-making through interactive reporting.
